# Exercise 7: The Path
**Chapter:** 6 (Healing and Transformation)
**Phase:** Experiencing pure Self energy
**Time:** 15-25 minutes

## Purpose
Leave all your parts behind and experience what it's like to be pure Self -- embodied, spacious, and vibrating with Self energy. This is one of the most powerful exercises in the book for accessing a felt sense of who you really are beneath the parts.

## Full Exercise

Get comfortable, and take deep breaths if that helps. In your mind's eye, put yourself at the base of a path. It could be one you've already been on or it could be totally new. Meet with your parts at the base of this path and ask them if they'd be willing to wait there for you and let you go on this brief journey by yourself for a little while.

Notice how they react. You can try to see if those who are afraid could be comforted by those who aren't. Let them all know that you won't be gone long and this would be good for them and for you, but they don't have to let you do it if they're not ready. Some days they're okay with it and some days they're not, and that's natural. If they're not up for it, spend the time getting to know them and their fears about letting you try this.

If they are up for it, head out on the path, reminding them that you'll be back soon.

As you continue on the path, notice if you're thinking anything at all, because if you are thinking, that means there's still some parts with you. See if they, too, would be willing to separate and go back with the others. If not, what's their fear?

You can also scan your body for anything that doesn't feel quite like you as your Self. If you find anything, that also is likely to be a part, and you can ask it to return to the base.

If the parts are willing to return, you'll gradually notice yourself becoming more and more pure awareness, without a lot of thought.

**Important:** If at any point you find yourself watching yourself taking this journey, that means there's a part trying to do it for you. Who is it that's watching? Ask that part to go to the base too, so that as you're on the path you don't see yourself -- you see your surroundings in a direct, first-person sort of way.

If your parts are really trusting you, by now you should be experiencing some of the qualities: clarity, the absence of thought, spaciousness, present centeredness, a sense of well-being, connectedness, being in your body, confidence. You may also sense a vibrating energy running through your body. That is Self energy. If you feel that energy, invite it to run throughout your body.

If you notice any places where the energy can't flow, it's likely there is a part blocking it. See if that part will go back to the base too.

At some point, just pause and take in this experience. Notice what it's like to have this much Self in your body. Notice the different ways you can feel it, as well as how and where Self energy manifests and reveals itself. Remember these markers -- that's how you can tell you're embodied as you go through your day.

**Optional:** If you are feeling a lot of Self energy, you have the option of inviting any messages from the universe. Maybe nothing comes, and that's okay, but people will sometimes get clear guidance at a point like this.

Now head back toward the base at whatever pace feels right. When you get back to your parts, see how they react to your return and thank them for whatever risks they took. Ask what it's been like for them. Check to see if they'll be up for letting you try it again sometime.

If you're still holding any tingling Self energy, you can extend it to your parts. It is very healing and you can actually direct it to your parts and to other people. Notice how your parts react to receiving the Self energy you have to offer them.

When all that feels complete, thank your parts once again and begin shifting your focus back outside. See if you can hold some of this Self state even as you open your eyes and come back.

## Key Takeaways
- Some people don't get far down the path -- that's not failure, it's data about what parts need more trust-building
- Self energy arises spontaneously once parts let you be fully embodied
- You can direct Self energy to parts and to other people
- This exercise often forces you to notice "Self-like managers" -- parts that are so blended they seem like Self but have protective agendas
- The experience of pure Self is not ethereal -- it's specific and palpable
